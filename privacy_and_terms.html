Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 19, 2026

Geo Silent is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, and secure your personal and location data when you use the Geo Silent mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

In order to provide our core service, we collect and process the following information:

  • Precise Location Data: We collect precise real-time location data (GPS coordinates) to determine when you enter or exit your configured silent zones.
  • Background Location Access: Because the primary feature of Geo Silent is to automatically silence or change your phone's ringer mode when you arrive at a specific location, the app requires location access in the background. Location data is monitored in the background even when the app is closed, not in use, or running in the background.
  • Account Credentials: When you register an account, we collect basic details such as your name, email address, and secure authentication tokens.

2. How We Use Your Data

We process your data for the following specific purposes:

  • To verify your identity and authenticate your account.
  • To save and synchronize your silent zone configurations securely to Firebase Firestore.
  • To trigger native system calls (Silent, Vibrate, or Normal ringer modes) when you enter or exit defined geographic zones.

3. Data Sharing & Disclosure

Your privacy is our highest priority. We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your location data or personal credentials with third-party advertisers, marketers, or analytics companies. Your location coordinates are processed locally on-device for ringer control and synchronized securely via encrypted connections to Firebase Firestore solely for syncing across your devices.

4. Data Security & Retention

All zone data and location records are synchronized using secure HTTPS connections. Location history is stored temporarily on-device and is not permanently logged on our servers. You have the right to request deletion of your data at any time.

5. User Choice & Deletion

You can manage, edit, or delete any silent zone directly inside the app. If you wish to delete all your personal data, you can permanently delete your account and all associated zones using the "Delete Account" button in the Privacy & Security settings. This action immediately and permanently removes all your data from our Firestore database.
